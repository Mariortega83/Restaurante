Essence

Essence is a culinary haven where tradition meets innovation. Founded by Martin Berasategi, a visionary chef with a passion for blending global flavors, the restaurant invites guests on a journey through unique, artfully crafted dishes. The menu changes seasonally, highlighting the freshest ingredients and the creative spirit behind each plate. With an ambiance that fuses modern elegance and warm hospitality, Essence promises an unforgettable dining experience.

At Essence, every detail is thoughtfully curated—from the subtle notes of decor that evoke both comfort and sophistication, to the impeccable service that ensures each guest feels at home. Our commitment to sustainability and local sourcing further enriches the story of each dish, making every visit not just a meal, but an exploration of taste and culture.

Martin Berasategi

Essence was founded by Martin Berasategui with the vision of creating a space where tradition meets innovation. After years of traveling and studying culinary arts in various countries, John felt inspired to blend the techniques and flavors he had encountered into a unique dining experience. Starting with a small, intimate venue, Essence initially gained recognition for its bold approach to reimagining classic dishes while honoring their roots.

The restaurant’s philosophy—capturing the "essence" of each ingredient—quickly resonated with diners and critics alike. Through meticulous attention to sourcing the finest seasonal ingredients and crafting visually stunning plates, Martin and his team built a reputation for excellence. Within a few years, Essence earned its first Michelin star, solidifying its place as a culinary landmark.
