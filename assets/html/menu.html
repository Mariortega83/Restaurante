Main Dishes

Heirloom Tomatoes with Orange Foam

This dish features a selection of heirloom tomatoes, carefully chosen for their sweet flavor and vibrant color, paired with a light orange foam, created using the technique of foaming. The tomatoes, thinly sliced, are complemented with a touch of sea salt and extra virgin olive oil to enhance their natural flavor. The orange foam, smooth and refreshing, adds a citrusy note that balances the sweetness of the tomatoes, creating a harmonious blend of fresh and tangy flavors. Garnished with microgreens and a dash of freshly ground black pepper, this dish is an explosion of textures and flavors that invites you to savor the purity of the ingredients, elevated by the sophistication of modern cuisine.

Crema Catalana

Crema catalana is a traditional Catalan dessert, similar to the French crème brûlée. It consists of a smooth and velvety custard made with egg yolks, sugar, milk, cinnamon, and lemon zest, which is gently cooked until it reaches a creamy consistency. The defining feature of crema catalana is its crispy caramelized sugar crust, formed by brûléing sugar on top using a torch or under a broiler. This contrast between the creamy texture of the custard and the crunchy sugar topping is what makes crema catalana so beloved.

Chocolate Symphony with Strawberry Essence

This exquisite dessert features a harmonious blend of fresh strawberries, rich chocolate, and an artistic touch of edible floral design. At its core, a generous layer of fresh, ripe strawberries is artfully arranged, their natural sweetness elevated by a silky, dark chocolate ganache that delicately coats each berry. The chocolate ganache is smooth and velvety, providing a deep, intense contrast to the fresh fruit.

Citrus-Infused Shrimp Carpaccio with Caviar and Orange Perles

This refined creation elevates the essence of seafood with the delicacy of shrimp carpaccio, crowned with the elegance of caviar. Molecular orange perles provide an exquisite citrus burst, balancing the richness of the shrimp and the briny touch of caviar, creating a harmonious fusion of textures and flavors that embody the art of contemporary fine dining.
